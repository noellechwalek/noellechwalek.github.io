My favorite video game of all time is...

Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Image Credit: Nintendo 2017

Why is Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild my favorite video game you may be wondering?

I love the open world of BoTW and the storyline. I love that you are able to explore the map freely without being tied down to a certain path. The main storyline is amazing as well as the side quests as well. There are so many new characters introduced who are all unique in their own way. I could play this game for hours on end without getting bored. The graphics of this game are jaw-dropping as well.

Here is a list of runner ups (in no particular order):

  1. Grand Theft Auto V
  2. Animal Crossings: New Horizons
  3. Stardew Valley
  4. The Sims
  5. Super Mario Party
  6. Fortnite
